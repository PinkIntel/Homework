A brief history

Anime is an abbreviation for "animation." It's roughly translated to "animated cartoon" in Japanese. It's a form of entertainment that includes many types of genres, such as romance, comedy, action, adventure, tragedy, historical fiction, and much more. Key characteristics of what makes anime different from cartoons is their art style. Characters are depicted with large eyes, wild hair, bold line art, and exaggerated features and expressions. Anime also has a lot of dramatic closeups, intense lighting and vibrant colors.

Anime has been noted to be popular because of its storytelling and memorable characters.

Just because it looks like a "cartoon" doesn't mean it's for kids. Some anime contain violence, crude language or even adult content.
